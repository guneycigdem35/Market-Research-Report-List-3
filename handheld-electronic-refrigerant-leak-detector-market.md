<p><p>Handheld Electronic Refrigerant Leak Detectors come in different types such as Infrared and Semiconductor. Infrared detectors use infrared technology to detect refrigerant leaks by sensing the gas molecules in the air. Semiconductor detectors, on the other hand, rely on semiconductor sensors to identify refrigerant leaks by measuring changes in electrical conductivity when in contact with the gas. Both types of detectors are widely used in the HVAC industry for efficient and effective leak detection in refrigeration systems.</p></p>
