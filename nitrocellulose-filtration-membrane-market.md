<p><p>Nitrocellulose Filtration Membrane market is categorized into three types based on pore sizes - Below 0.45 µm, 0.45 to 6 µm, and above 6 µm. Below 0.45 µm pore sizes membranes are used for ultrafiltration of small molecules, proteins, and viruses. Membranes with pore sizes ranging from 0.45 to 6 µm are suitable for microfiltration applications for bacteria and larger particles. Membranes with above 6 µm pore sizes are used for coarse filtration applications such as cell harvesting and clarification processes.</p></p>
