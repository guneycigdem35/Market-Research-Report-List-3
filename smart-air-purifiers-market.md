<p><p>Smart air purifiers come in various types including HEPA filters, Electrostatic Precipitators, Ionizers, and Ozone Generators. HEPA filters are known for their high efficiency in removing airborne particles. Electrostatic precipitators use electrostatic charges to trap particles in the air. Ionizers release negatively charged ions to attract pollutants. Ozone generators produce ozone to neutralize odors and kill bacteria. Each type of air purifier offers unique benefits in improving indoor air quality through advanced technologies.</p></p>
