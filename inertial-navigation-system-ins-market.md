<p><p>Inertial Navigation System (INS) market encompasses different types of technologies including Mechanical Gyro, Ring Laser Gyro, Fiber Optics Gyro, and MEMS Technology. Mechanical Gyro technology relies on spinning wheels to track orientation changes. Ring Laser Gyro uses lasers to measure rotation. Fiber Optics Gyro utilizes light interference patterns to calculate movement. MEMS Technology consists of tiny sensors that detect acceleration and rotation. These diverse technologies cater to various industries requiring precise navigation and positioning systems.</p></p>
