<p><p>Limb prosthetics market can be divided into two main types: lower limb prosthetics and upper limb prosthetics. Lower limb prosthetics are artificial limbs designed to replace lost or missing legs, providing support and mobility to individuals who have undergone amputations below the waist. On the other hand, upper limb prosthetics are designed to replace lost or missing arms, hands, or fingers, allowing individuals to regain functionality and perform daily tasks. Both lower limb and upper limb prosthetics play a crucial role in improving the quality of life for amputees.</p></p>
