<p><p>Gamma detectors are used to detect gamma radiation in various industries such as healthcare, nuclear power, and environmental monitoring. The market for gamma detectors is segmented into three main types: scintillation detectors, semiconductor-based detectors, and other detectors. Scintillation detectors use a crystal material to convert gamma radiation into light pulses, semiconductor-based detectors use semiconducting materials to detect gamma radiation, and other detectors include gas-filled detectors and proportional counters. Each type of detector offers unique advantages and is used for different applications within the gamma detection market.</p></p>
