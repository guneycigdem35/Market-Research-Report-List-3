<p><p>Horizontal metal balers are used for compacting and bundling metal scrap for easy transportation and recycling. There are two main types of horizontal metal balers in the market- manual and PLC automatic. Manual balers require operators to manually control the compression process, while PLC automatic balers use programmable logic controllers to automate the operation. PLC automatic balers are more efficient and require less human intervention, making them popular for industrial applications where high volume processing is needed.</p></p>
