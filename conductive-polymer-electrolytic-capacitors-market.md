<p><p>Conductive Polymer Electrolytic Capacitors come in two main types: Single Ended and Surface Mount. Single Ended capacitors have one lead connected to the anode and the other to the cathode, making them ideal for simple applications. Surface Mount capacitors are designed to be mounted directly onto circuit boards, saving space and reducing assembly time. These capacitors are widely used in various industries such as automotive, electronics, and telecommunications due to their high capacitance, low equivalent series resistance (ESR), and long lifespan.</p></p>
