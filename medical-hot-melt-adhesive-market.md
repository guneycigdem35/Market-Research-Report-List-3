<p><p>Medical hot melt adhesive is a type of adhesive commonly used in the medical industry for joining different medical components or devices. The adhesive melts when exposed to heat and is then applied to join the surfaces together, providing a strong bond. Medical hot melt adhesives are especially popular due to their biocompatibility, sterilization resistance, and ability to bond different materials commonly used in medical devices.</p><p>The Medical Hot Melt Adhesive Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.40% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing use of medical devices and components, especially with the growing aging population and technological advancements in the medical industry. Additionally, the rise in minimally invasive surgeries and the need for reliable and safe bonding solutions further drive the demand for medical hot melt adhesives.</p><p>The latest trends in the Medical Hot Melt Adhesive Market include the development of bio-based hot melt adhesives to cater to the increasing demand for environmentally friendly products.
