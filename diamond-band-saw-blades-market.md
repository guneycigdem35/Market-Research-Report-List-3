<p><p>Diamond band saw blades can be categorized into two main types: Toothless Type and Tooth Type. Toothless Type blades do not have teeth, instead they utilize a continuous rim of diamond particles for cutting. These blades are ideal for cutting delicate materials with precision. On the other hand, Tooth Type blades have teeth that are coated with diamond particles for aggressive cutting of hard materials. Both types are commonly used in industries such as glass cutting, stone cutting, and metalworking for high precision and efficiency.</p></p>
