<p><p>Film developing services are typically categorized into three main market types: black and white negative processing, colour processing, and other specialty services. Black and white negative processing involves developing black and white film to produce monochromatic images, while colour processing involves developing colour film to produce vibrant, full-colour images. Other specialty services may include services such as slide film processing, medium format film processing, and cross-processing, which involves developing film in chemicals meant for a different type of film to create unique and unpredictable results.</p></p>
