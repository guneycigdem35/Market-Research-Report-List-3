Frothers are surfactants that help create a stable froth to carry the minerals to the surface. Modifiers are chemicals that can alter the characteristics of the froth to improve separation efficiency. These chemicals play crucial roles in the froth flotation process, which is used in the mining industry for separating valuable minerals from ore.</p></p>
