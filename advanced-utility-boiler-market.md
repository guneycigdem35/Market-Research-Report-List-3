<p><p>The Advanced Utility Boiler market can be categorized into different types based on their operational characteristics. Subcritical boilers operate at pressures below the critical point of water, while Supercritical boilers operate at pressures above the critical point, leading to higher efficiency. Ultra Supercritical boilers operate at even higher temperatures and pressures, offering even greater efficiency. Other types in the market include Fluidized Bed Boilers and Pulverized Coal Boilers. Each type of boiler offers unique advantages and is used in different applications based on their specific features.</p></p>
