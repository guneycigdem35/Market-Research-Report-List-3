<p><p>Coal Fired Generation Market involves two main types of technologies: PCC (Pulverized Coal Combustion) and Cyclone Furnace. PCC technology involves grinding coal into a fine powder before burning it in a boiler to produce steam, while Cyclone Furnace technology involves mixing coal with high-speed air and burning it in a cyclone-shaped chamber. Both technologies are widely used in coal-fired power plants to generate electricity, with PCC being more common due to its higher efficiency and lower emissions compared to Cyclone Furnace.</p></p>
