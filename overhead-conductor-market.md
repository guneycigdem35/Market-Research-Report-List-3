<p><p>An overhead conductor, also known as a transmission line conductor, is a type of electrical conductor that is used to transmit electricity over long distances. <p><p>Conventional Overhead Conductor systems are traditional power transmission lines made of aluminum or steel conductors. These lines are suitable for moderate temperature environments. High-Temperature Overhead Conductors, on the other hand, are designed to withstand higher temperatures, allowing for efficient power transmission over longer distances.
