<p><p>Bi-axially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) is a type of polypropylene film that is stretched in two directions to create a stronger, more flexible material. There are two main types of BOPP production methods: the flat film strength method and the tubular quench method. The flat film strength method involves stretching the film in both directions on a flat surface, while the tubular quench method involves cooling the film as it is extruded through a circular die. Both methods result in high-quality BOPP films used in various packaging applications.</p></p>
