<p><p>Painless Plug Market offers two main types: Collagen and Silicone. Collagen plugs are made from natural protein fibers and are known for their biocompatibility and ability to gradually absorb into the body. Silicone plugs, on the other hand, are made from a synthetic material and provide longer-lasting relief by forming a barrier to prevent tear evaporation. Both types of plugs are used in the treatment of dry eye syndrome and other corneal conditions, offering patients a painless and effective solution for their eye care needs.</p></p>
