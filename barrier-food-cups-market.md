Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) offers transparency and heat resistance. Biaxial Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) is widely used for its high clarity and stiffness. Polystyrene (PS) is known for its rigidity and affordability. Other materials such as PET and PLA also serve specific applications in the barrier food cup market.</p></p>
