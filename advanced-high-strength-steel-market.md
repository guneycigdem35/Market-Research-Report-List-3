<p><p>Advanced High-strength Steel (AHSS) market includes various types such as Dual Phase (DP), Transformation-induced Plasticity (TRIP), Complex Phase (CP), and others. DP steel offers a balance of strength and formability, TRIP steel provides high ductility and energy absorption, CP steel has a combination of strength and formability. Other types include Martensitic, Boron, and Twinning-Induced Plasticity (TWIP) steels. These AHSS types cater to different applications in the automotive, construction, and manufacturing industries due to their unique properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
