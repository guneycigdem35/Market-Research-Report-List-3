<p><strong>The Angiography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Ray Angiography</li><li>CT Angiography</li><li>MR Angiography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Angiography equipment is separated into three main types: X-Ray Angiography, CT Angiography, and MR Angiography. X-Ray Angiography uses a contrast dye and X-ray technology to create detailed images of blood vessels. CT Angiography uses a CT scanner to generate 3D images of blood vessels. MR Angiography uses magnetic resonance imaging to create detailed images of blood vessels without using ionizing radiation. Each type of angiography equipment has its own advantages and is used in different clinical scenarios based on the patient's needs and medical conditions.</p></p>
