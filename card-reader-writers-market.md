<p><p>The card reader-writer market includes various types such as contact card reader-writers, contactless card reader-writers, biometric card reader-writers, and magnetic-stripe card reader-writers. Contact card reader-writers require physical contact with the card for data transfer, while contactless ones operate through radio frequency. Biometric card reader-writers use unique biological traits for authentication. Magnetic-stripe card reader-writers read information from magnetic stripes on cards. Each type caters to specific needs and preferences, offering different levels of security and convenience for users.</p></p>
