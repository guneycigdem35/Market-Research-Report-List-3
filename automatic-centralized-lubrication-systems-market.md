<p><p>Automatic lubrication systems automatically deliver the correct amount of lubricant to multiple lubrication points on a machine or equipment, reducing manual lubrication labor and improving equipment reliability. There are two main types of automatic lubrication systems: grease lubrication and oil lubrication systems. Grease lubrication systems use grease to lubricate components, while oil lubrication systems use oil. Both systems are centralized, meaning they distribute lubricant from a central reservoir to various points on the equipment, ensuring proper lubrication at all times.</p></p>
