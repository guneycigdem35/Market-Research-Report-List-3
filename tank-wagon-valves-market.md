<p><strong>The Tank Wagon Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emergency Valves</li><li>Bottom Valves</li><li>Belly Valves</li><li>Internal Valves</li><li>Sump Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tank wagon valves are categorized into different types based on their placement and function. Emergency valves are used for quick shut off in case of emergencies. Bottom valves are located at the bottom of the tank for easy drainage. Belly valves are situated on the side of the tank for efficient liquid transfer. Internal valves are installed inside the tank for controlling flow. Sump valves are designed to drain the lowest point in the tank. Each type serves a specific purpose in the safe and efficient operation of tank wagons.</p></p>
