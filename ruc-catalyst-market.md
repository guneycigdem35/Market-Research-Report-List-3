<p><p>Ru/C catalyst market can be classified into non-homogeneous and homogeneous types based on the distribution of ruthenium and carbon components within the catalyst. Non-homogeneous Ru/C catalysts have uneven distribution of ruthenium and carbon throughout the catalyst, leading to variations in catalytic activity. On the other hand, homogeneous Ru/C catalysts have a uniform distribution of ruthenium and carbon, resulting in consistent catalytic performance. Both types cater to different applications and industries, depending on the desired level of performance and efficiency.</p></p>
