<p><p>NRT smoking cessation aids are available in various forms such as sprays, inhalers, chewing gums, transdermal patches, and sublingual tablets. Sprays deliver nicotine through the lining of the mouth, inhalers simulate the act of smoking while delivering nicotine, chewing gums release nicotine slowly when chewed, transdermal patches provide a continuous dose of nicotine through the skin, and sublingual tablets are placed under the tongue for absorption. These different types cater to individual preferences and needs in quitting smoking.</p></p>
