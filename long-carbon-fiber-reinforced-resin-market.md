<p><p>Long Carbon Fiber Reinforced Resin market can be categorized into two types: Thermosetting Type and Thermoplastics Type. Thermosetting Type is a type of resin that hardens irreversibly when heated and does not soften when reheated, making it ideal for high temperature applications. Thermoplastics Type, on the other hand, softens and hardens in a reversible manner with temperature changes, allowing for easy molding and recycling. Both types offer high strength and lightweight properties, making them popular choices for various industries including automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
