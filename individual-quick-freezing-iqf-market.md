<p><p>Individual Quick Freezing (IQF) is a method of freezing food products quickly and individually to maintain their quality. Mechanical IQF uses air blast or plate freezers to freeze products, while Cryogenic IQF involves the use of liquid nitrogen or carbon dioxide to rapidly freeze products. Mechanical IQF is more cost-effective and widely used, while Cryogenic IQF offers faster freezing rates and better product quality. Both methods are popular in the food industry for preserving the taste, texture, and nutritional value of frozen foods.</p></p>
