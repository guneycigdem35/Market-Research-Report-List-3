<p><p>PVC-C Pipes are commonly available in two types: Schedule 40 and Schedule 80. Schedule 40 pipes are designed for general purpose applications where light to medium duty is required, such as residential plumbing and irrigation. On the other hand, Schedule 80 pipes are thicker and stronger, suitable for heavy-duty applications, including industrial settings and underground installations. While Schedule 40 pipes are more affordable and easier to work with, Schedule 80 pipes offer increased durability and are able to withstand higher pressure and temperature conditions.</p></p>
