Disk Phantoms are flat and disk-shaped, often used for verifying dose distributions in radiotherapy. Cylindrical Phantoms, as the name suggests, are cylindrical in shape and are used for simulating various body tissues and organs in radiation therapy quality assurance.</p></p>
