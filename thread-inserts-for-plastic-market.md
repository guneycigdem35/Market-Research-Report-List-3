<p><strong>The Thread Inserts for Plastic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tanged Thread Inserts</li><li>Tangless Thread Inserts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tanged Thread Inserts are designed with tangs that provide a locking feature to hold the insert in place during installation in plastic materials. They are commonly used in applications where strong torque and pull-out resistance are required. Tangless Thread Inserts, on the other hand, do not have tangs and rely on a coiled design to provide a secure fit in plastic materials. They are preferred for applications where tang breakage is a concern and offer easy installation with reduced risk of tang removal.</p></p>
