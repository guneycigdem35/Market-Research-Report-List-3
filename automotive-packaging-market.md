<p><p>Automotive packaging includes two main types: expendable packaging and returnable packaging. Expendable packaging is designed for one-time use and is typically made of materials like cardboard or foam. This type of packaging is cost-effective but not as environmentally friendly as returnable packaging. Returnable packaging, on the other hand, is reusable and durable, often made of materials like plastic or metal. While returnable packaging may require a higher upfront investment, it can be more sustainable and cost-effective in the long run.</p></p>
