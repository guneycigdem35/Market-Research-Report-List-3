<p><p>Semiconductor heat treatment furnaces are widely used in the semiconductor industry for various thermal processes. Horizontal furnaces have a flat layout where wafers are loaded horizontally for heat treatment, offering high throughput and uniform temperature distribution. Vertical furnaces, on the other hand, have a vertical orientation where wafers are loaded vertically, allowing for better control over gas flow and improved thermal uniformity. Each type of furnace caters to specific needs in the semiconductor industry, providing efficient and precise thermal processing capabilities.</p></p>
