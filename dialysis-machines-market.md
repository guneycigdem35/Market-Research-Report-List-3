There are two main types of dialysis machines: Hemodialysis and Peritoneal Dialysis. Hemodialysis machines filter blood outside the body through a dialyzer, while Peritoneal Dialysis machines use the patient's peritoneal cavity as a filter. Each market type offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the patient's medical needs and preferences.</p></p>
