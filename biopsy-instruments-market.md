<p><p>Biopsy instruments market can be categorized into three main types: core needle biopsy, aspiration biopsy, and vacuum assisted biopsy. Core needle biopsy involves removing a sample tissue using a hollow needle, aspiration biopsy involves extracting fluid or cells using a syringe, and vacuum assisted biopsy utilizes suction to collect larger tissue samples. Each type offers different benefits and is used based on the specific needs of the patient and the suspected condition being investigated.</p></p>
