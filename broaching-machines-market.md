<p><p>Broaching machines are classified into two main types based on their orientation: vertical broaching machines and horizontal broaching machines. Vertical broaching machines are designed to cut or remove material vertically, which is ideal for internal keyways and splines. On the other hand, horizontal broaching machines cut or remove material horizontally, making them suitable for long or continuous cuts. Both types of broaching machines offer unique capabilities and are commonly used in various industries for precision machining applications.</p></p>
