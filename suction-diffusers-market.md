<p><p>Suction diffusers are devices used in piping systems to remove debris and prevent clogging of pumps. Integral vane suction diffusers have the vanes built into the body of the diffuser, while separate vane suction diffusers have removable vanes that can be cleaned or replaced easily. Integral vane diffusers are preferred for their compact design and ease of installation, while separate vane diffusers offer more flexibility for maintenance and customization. Both types are commonly used in various industrial applications to improve the efficiency and longevity of pumping systems.</p></p>
