<p><p>Vertical cartoning machines are used in packaging products in cartons. Two common types of vertical cartoning machines are end load cartoners and top load cartoners. End load cartoners assemble cartons around the product from the side, while top load cartoners place the product into a pre-formed carton from the top. End load cartoners are efficient for packaging items that are already in a tray or case, while top load cartoners are ideal for products that need to be placed into a carton vertically.</p></p>
