<p><ul><li>Plug-in Relay</li><li>PCB Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric vehicle relays market can be divided into two main types: Plug-in Relay and PCB Relay. Plug-in relays are devices that can be easily plugged and unplugged from a socket on a circuit board, making them convenient for maintenance and replacement. On the other hand, PCB relays are soldered directly onto the circuit board, providing a more permanent and compact solution. Both types of relays play a crucial role in the functioning of electric vehicles by controlling various electrical components and ensuring smooth operation.</p></p>
