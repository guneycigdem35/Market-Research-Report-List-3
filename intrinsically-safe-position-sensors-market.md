<p><p>Intrinsically safe position sensors are divided into four main types: Guidance Type, Insertion Type, Straight Line Type, and L Type. Guidance Type sensors provide guidance for objects or machinery in motion. Insertion Type sensors are inserted into a specific location to detect position changes. Straight Line Type sensors detect linear movements in a straight line. L Type sensors are designed to detect positions in a specific L-shaped orientation. Each type has its own unique applications and advantages in industries where safety is a top priority.</p></p>
