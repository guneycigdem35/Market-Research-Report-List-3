<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) Earphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Feedforward Active Noise Canceling</li><li>Feedback ANC</li><li>Hybrid ANC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Feedforward Active Noise Canceling: In this type of ANC, a microphone placed outside the earphone picks up the external noise and produces an anti-noise signal to cancel it out before reaching the ears of the user.</p><p>Feedback ANC: Feedback ANC uses a microphone inside the earphone to pick up the residual noise that gets past the earphone. It then produces an anti-noise signal to cancel out this residual noise.</p><p>Hybrid ANC: Hybrid ANC combines both feedforward and feedback ANC techniques to provide even better noise cancellation by actively reducing both external and internal noise.</p></p>
