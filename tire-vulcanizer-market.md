<p><ul><li>Mechanical vulcanizer</li><li>Hydraulic vulcanizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tire vulcanizers come in two main types: mechanical and hydraulic. Mechanical vulcanizers use mechanical force to apply pressure and heat to the tire during the vulcanization process. They are generally simpler in design and operation compared to hydraulic vulcanizers. On the other hand, hydraulic vulcanizers use hydraulic pressure to apply force, making them more efficient and precise in controlling the vulcanization process. Both types are widely used in the tire manufacturing industry for curing and shaping tires.</p></p>
