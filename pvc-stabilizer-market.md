<p><p>PVC stabilizers are additives used to prevent the degradation of PVC during processing and use. The four main types of PVC stabilizers are Lead Stabilizers, Mixed Metal Stabilizers, Tin Stabilizers, and Organic Stabilizers. Lead stabilizers are cost-effective and provide good heat stability. Mixed metal stabilizers offer excellent thermal and light stability. Tin stabilizers have good transparency and heat stability. Organic stabilizers are eco-friendly and offer high heat stability. Each type of stabilizer offers unique benefits for different applications in the PVC market.</p></p>
