<p><p>The insulin preparation market can be categorized into three main types: fast-acting, intermediate-acting, and long-acting insulin. Fast-acting insulin is designed to quickly lower blood sugar levels after meals. Intermediate-acting insulin helps control blood sugar throughout the day and night. Long-acting insulin provides a steady release of insulin over an extended period of time, offering consistent blood sugar control. Each type of insulin preparation caters to different needs and preferences of individuals with diabetes.</p></p>
