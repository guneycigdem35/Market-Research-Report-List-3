<p><p>Pipettor tips come in two main market types: Non-Filtered Pipette Tips and Filtered Pipette Tips. Non-Filtered Pipette Tips are used for standard liquid handling applications without any additional filtration. Filtered Pipette Tips, on the other hand, contain built-in filters that prevent aerosols and liquids from contaminating the pipettor or sample during aspiration and dispensing. Both types offer specific benefits depending on the application, with Filtered Pipette Tips being ideal for applications requiring additional protection from contamination.</p></p>
