<p><p>Medical fiber optics market can be categorized into two types: Silica fiber optics (also known as glass fiber optics) and plastic fiber optics. Silica fiber optics are made from high-purity glass, offering high transmission efficiency and low signal loss, making them ideal for medical imaging and diagnostics. On the other hand, plastic fiber optics are more flexible and cost-effective, suitable for applications like endoscopy and light therapy. Both types of fiber optics play a crucial role in advancing medical technologies and improving patient care.</p></p>
