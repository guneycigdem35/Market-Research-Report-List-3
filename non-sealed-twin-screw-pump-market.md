<p><p>Non-sealed Twin-Screw Pump market can be divided into two main types: Single Volute Twin Screw Pump and Double Volutes Twin Screw Pump. </p><p>Single Volute Twin Screw Pump features a single volute casing design, which directs the flow of liquid through the pump. This design is suitable for applications requiring high efficiency and low pulsation.</p><p>Double Volutes Twin Screw Pump, on the other hand, has two volute casings, which helps reduce axial forces and vibrations, making it ideal for high-pressure applications or when handling viscous fluids.</p></p>
