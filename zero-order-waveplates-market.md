<p><p>Zero-Order Waveplates are optical components that offer precise control over the polarization state of light. Two common types are Zero-Order Half-Wave Waveplates and Zero-Order Quarter-Wave Waveplates. Half-wave waveplates retard the phase of one polarization component by half a wavelength, while quarter-wave waveplates retard the phase by a quarter of a wavelength. These waveplates find applications in various industries such as telecommunications, optical instrumentation, and medical devices due to their ability to manipulate and control the polarization of light with high precision.</p></p>
