External Gear Pump works by using two external gears to pump fluid, while Internal Gear Pump utilizes an internal and external gear to create suction. Gear Ring Pump involves a series of internal gears working together, and Screw Spindle Pump uses a helical screw to move fluid. Each type offers different advantages and applications in the hydraulic pump market.</p></p>
