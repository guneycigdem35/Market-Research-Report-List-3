<p><p>Disposable manual resuscitators are used for providing artificial ventilation in emergency situations. There are two main types of disposable manual resuscitators on the market: self-inflating resuscitators and flow-inflating resuscitators. Self-inflating resuscitators consist of a bag that inflates on its own, making them easy to use in quick response scenarios. Flow-inflating resuscitators require a gas source to inflate the bag, allowing for greater control over the amount of oxygen delivered to the patient. Both types are essential tools in respiratory care.</p></p>
