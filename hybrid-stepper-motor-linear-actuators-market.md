<p><p>Hybrid stepper motor linear actuators come in three main types:</p><p>1. Fixed Shaft Type: These actuators have a fixed output shaft and are commonly used in applications where the load is directly coupled to the shaft.</p><p>2. Straight Shaft Type: These actuators have a straight-through design without any curves or bends in the shaft, making them ideal for applications requiring precise linear motion.</p><p>3. Externally Driven: These actuators are driven by an external source, such as a belt or pulley system, allowing for greater flexibility and customization in the motion control system.</p></p>
