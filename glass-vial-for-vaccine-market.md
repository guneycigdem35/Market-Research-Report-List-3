<p><p>The Glass Vial for Vaccine Market comprises two main types: Molded Glass Vials and Tubular Glass Vials. Molded Glass Vials are made by molding glass into a specific shape, allowing for customization of the vial design. Tubular Glass Vials are formed by blowing molten glass into a tube shape, which is then cut and molded into individual vials. Both types of vials are widely used in the pharmaceutical industry for storing and delivering vaccines safely and efficiently.</p></p>
