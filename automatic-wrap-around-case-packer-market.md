<p><p>The automatic wrap around case packer market is divided into two main types: Continuous and Intermittent. Continuous case packers operate without stopping, allowing for a continuous flow of products into the packaging process. Intermittent case packers work in cycles, stopping and starting between each packaging operation. Continuous case packers are ideal for high-speed production lines that require constant packaging output, while intermittent case packers are better suited for slower production lines that can accommodate brief stopping periods between packaging cycles.</p></p>
