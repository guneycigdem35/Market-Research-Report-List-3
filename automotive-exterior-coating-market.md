<p><p>Automotive exterior coating market consists of two main types: water-based paint and oil paint. Water-based paint is environmentally friendly, as it has lower levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and is easier to clean up. On the other hand, oil paint provides a more durable and glossy finish but contains higher levels of VOCs. Both types of coatings are used in automotive applications to protect the exterior of vehicles from corrosion, weathering, and scratches, as well as to enhance their appearance.</p></p>
