<p><ul><li>Two-pole Motor</li><li>Four-pole Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power seat actuator motors are classified into two types: two-pole motors and four-pole motors. Two-pole motors have two magnetic poles in the stator, providing higher speed and efficiency. On the other hand, four-pole motors have four magnetic poles, offering smoother operation and better torque. While two-pole motors are suitable for applications where speed is critical, four-pole motors are better suited for applications where a balance of speed and torque is required. Both types cater to different requirements in the power seat actuator motor market.</p></p>
