<p><p>Fillable autoinjectors are devices that allow patients to manually fill the device with their prescribed medication before injection. These devices offer flexibility in dosing and medication choice. On the other hand, prefilled autoinjectors come pre-filled with a specific medication, eliminating the need for manual filling and reducing the risk of dosing errors. Prefilled autoinjectors are convenient for patients and caregivers, as they are ready to use right out of the package. Both types of autoinjectors offer ease of use and convenience for patients requiring injections.</p></p>
