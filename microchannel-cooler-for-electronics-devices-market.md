<p><p>Microchannel cooler for electronics devices is divided into two main types: stackable type and array type. The stackable type consists of multiple layers of microchannels stacked on top of each other, allowing for increased cooling efficiency in a compact design. On the other hand, the array type features multiple parallel microchannels arranged in a grid pattern, providing effective heat dissipation across a larger surface area. Both types of microchannel coolers are essential for maintaining optimal operating temperatures in electronic devices.</p></p>
