<p><p>The Floor-Standing Sterilizer market encompasses various types such as Steam, Vacuum, UV, Ozone, High Temperature, and Others. Steam Floor-standing Sterilizers use high-pressure steam to kill bacteria and pathogens. Vacuum Floor-standing Sterilizers remove air from the chamber before sterilization. UV Floor-standing Sterilizers use ultraviolet light to disinfect surfaces. Ozone Floor-standing Sterilizers use ozone gas to kill germs. High Temperature Floor-standing Sterilizers rely on extreme heat for sterilization. Other types may include dry heat or ethylene oxide sterilizers. Each type offers unique benefits for different sterilization needs.</p></p>
