<p><p>Lead glass is a type of glass that is made by adding lead oxide to the glass mixture. In the construction industry, lead glass is used in windows and doors for its ability to provide sound insulation and energy efficiency. Additionally, the increasing demand for luxury glassware and home decor products is also driving the growth of the lead glass market.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the lead glass market include the development of lead-free alternatives to address environmental concerns, and the increasing adoption of lead glass in the automotive industry for windshield applications.
