<p><p>Time Temperature Indicators (TTIs) are used to monitor the temperature exposure of a product over a period of time. Full History Indicators provide a continuous record of temperature exposure from the moment of activation, while Partial History Indicators only show temperature breaches that have occurred in the past. Full History Indicators are preferred for products requiring strict temperature control throughout their entire journey, while Partial History Indicators are suitable for products where intermittent temperature monitoring is sufficient.</p></p>
