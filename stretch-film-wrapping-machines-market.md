<p><p>Stretch film wrapping machines are available in two main types: semi-automatic and automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention, such as loading the pallet onto the turntable and attaching the film to the pallet. In contrast, automatic machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human involvement as they can wrap pallets entirely on their own. Both types of machines are used in industrial settings to improve efficiency, ensure product protection, and streamline the packaging process.</p></p>
