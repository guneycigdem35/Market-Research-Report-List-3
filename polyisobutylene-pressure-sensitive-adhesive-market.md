<p><p>Polyisobutylene pressure sensitive adhesive is available in two main types in the market, waterborne type and solvent type. Waterborne type adhesive is made by dispersing polyisobutylene particles in water, making it environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. Solvent type adhesive, on the other hand, is made by dissolving polyisobutylene in organic solvents. While waterborne type adhesive is preferred for its eco-friendly properties, solvent type adhesive is known for its strong bonding capabilities and faster drying times.</p></p>
