<p><p>A biventricular pacemaker, also known as a cardiac resynchronization therapy (CRT) device, is a type of pacemaker that sends electrical signals to both ventricles of the heart to help them beat in a more synchronized manner. This can improve the heart's overall pumping efficiency, particularly in patients with heart failure or certain types of arrhythmias. Biventricular pacemakers are often recommended for patients who have not responded well to other treatments.</p><p>The Biventricular Pacemaker Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period.
