<p><strong>The VR and AR Optics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waveguide</li><li>Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VR and AR optics market consists of two main types - waveguide and lens. Waveguide technology involves using a transparent material to guide light from a projector to the user's eyes, creating immersive visuals in a compact form factor. On the other hand, lens technology uses specialized lenses to magnify and focus images, leading to high-quality visuals in VR and AR devices. Both waveguide and lens technologies play a crucial role in enhancing the user experience in virtual and augmented reality applications.</p></p>
