<p><p>Large motor graders can be classified into two main types based on their wheel configuration: six-wheel (three-axle) motor graders and four-wheel (two-axle) motor graders. Six-wheel motor graders typically have better stability and weight distribution due to the additional axle, making them suitable for larger construction projects and tougher terrain. On the other hand, four-wheel motor graders are more compact and maneuverable, making them ideal for smaller construction sites and urban areas where space is limited.</p></p>
