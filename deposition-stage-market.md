<p><p>The Deposition Stage market consists of two primary types: In-Line Substrate Heater Stage and Right Angle Substrate Heater Stage. In-Line Substrate Heater Stage is designed for continuous deposition processes, where substrates are heated in a straight line configuration. Right Angle Substrate Heater Stage, on the other hand, is specifically engineered for deposition processes that require substrates to be heated at a right angle position. Both market types cater to different deposition requirements and offer advanced heating capabilities for efficient thin film deposition.</p></p>
