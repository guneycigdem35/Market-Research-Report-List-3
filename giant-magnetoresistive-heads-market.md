<p><p>Film Type Giant Magnetoresistive Heads and Needle Type Giant Magnetoresistive Heads are two main types of heads used in hard disk drives. Film Type heads are constructed with multiple layers of thin films that enhance the sensitivity and performance of the head. On the other hand, Needle Type heads have a more traditional design with a sharp needle-like tip that reads data from the disk surface. Both types of heads have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
