<p><p>Lignosulfonate-based Concrete Admixtures are types of additives used in concrete to improve its strength, workability, and durability. These additives can be classified into various types including Air Entrained Agent, Swelling Agent, Waterproof Agent, and Antifreeze. Air Entrained Agents help to create tiny air bubbles in the concrete, improving its resistance to freeze-thaw cycles. Swelling Agents help to reduce shrinkage and cracking, while Waterproof Agents enhance the water resistance of concrete. Antifreeze agents are used in cold weather to prevent freezing and ensure proper curing of concrete.</p></p>
