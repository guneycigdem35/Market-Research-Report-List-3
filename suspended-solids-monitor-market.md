<p><strong>The Suspended Solids Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible Probe</li><li>Insert Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Suspended Solids Monitors come in two main types: Submersible Probe and Insert Probe. Submersible Probe monitors are designed to be placed directly in water bodies for continuous monitoring of suspended solids levels. Insert Probe monitors, on the other hand, are designed to be installed in pipes or tanks to provide real-time data on suspended solids within the liquid. Both types of monitors are important tools for industries such as wastewater treatment plants, food processing, and mining to ensure efficient and compliant operations.</p></p>
