<p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) devices come in two main types - Wearable and Implantable. Wearable CGM devices are typically worn on the body, providing real-time glucose readings throughout the day. They are convenient and non-invasive, making them popular among individuals with diabetes. On the other hand, Implantable CGM devices are surgically inserted under the skin, offering continuous glucose monitoring without the need for frequent sensor changes. While they may be more invasive, implantable devices provide long-term monitoring and can be more accurate in some cases.</p></p>
