<p><p>Joint reconstruction and replacement market includes various types such as joint replacement, osteotomy, arthroscopy, resurfacing, arthrodesis, and others. Joint replacement involves replacing a damaged joint with a prosthetic one. Osteotomy is a surgical procedure that involves cutting and reshaping bones to relieve pressure on a damaged joint. Arthroscopy is a minimally invasive procedure used to diagnose and treat joint problems. Resurfacing involves smoothing or replacing damaged joint surfaces. Arthrodesis is a procedure that fuses two bones in a joint together. Other procedures may include ligament repairs or cartilage restoration.</p></p>
