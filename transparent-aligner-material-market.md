<p><strong>The Transparent Aligner Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer TPU</li><li>Multi-layer TPU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transparent aligner materials are commonly categorized into two types: single layer TPU and multi-layer TPU. Single layer TPU aligners consist of a single layer of thermoplastic polyurethane material that is durable and transparent, providing a comfortable fit for users. On the other hand, multi-layer TPU aligners have additional layers that enhance the strength and flexibility of the aligner, making them suitable for more complex orthodontic cases. Both types of aligners offer effective teeth straightening solutions with varying levels of durability and flexibility.</p></p>
