<p><p>Implantable catheter ports are devices used for long-term access to the bloodstream for infusion of medications, fluids, or blood products. There are two main types of implantable catheter ports: titanium infusion ports and plastic infusion ports. Titanium infusion ports are durable and long-lasting, suitable for patients requiring frequent access. Plastic infusion ports are more cost-effective and commonly used in patients with a shorter treatment duration. Both types of ports provide a convenient and reliable way for healthcare providers to administer treatments to patients.</p></p>
