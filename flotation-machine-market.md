<p><p>Flotation machines are categorized into three main types - Pneumatic Flotation Machines, Mechanical Agitation Flotation Machines, and Others. Pneumatic flotation machines rely on air to generate bubbles and separate particles from the liquid. Mechanical agitation flotation machines use impellers to stir the liquid and create bubbles for separation. The Other category includes various types of flotation machines that utilize different mechanisms for particle separation. Each type of flotation machine has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
