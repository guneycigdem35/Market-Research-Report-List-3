<p><p>An Artificial Blood Vessel is a medical device that is used to replace or repair damaged or diseased blood vessels in the body. Artificial blood vessels are used in a variety of medical procedures, including coronary artery bypass surgery, dialysis access, and peripheral artery bypass surgery.</p><p>The global Artificial Blood Vessel Market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 3.41% during the forecast period.
