<p><p>Nitrogenous water soluble fertilizers are used in various agricultural applications such as foliar application, soilless culture, soaking seeds and dipping roots, sprinkling irrigation, and other methods. Foliar application involves spraying the fertilizer directly onto the leaves of plants for quick absorption. Soilless culture refers to growing plants without using soil, where the fertilizer is added to the water solution. Soaking seeds and dipping roots involves treating seeds or root systems with the fertilizer solution. Sprinkling irrigation involves applying the fertilizer through sprinkler systems.</p></p>
