<p><strong>The Disposable Ileostomy Bag Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Piece Bag</li><li>Two Piece Bag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disposable ileostomy bags are commonly available in two main types: one-piece bags and two-piece bags. One-piece bags consist of a single unit where the pouch and skin barrier are combined, making them easy to use and dispose of in one step. In contrast, two-piece bags have a separate pouch and skin barrier, allowing for more flexibility in changing either component without replacing the entire system. Both types provide convenience and discretion for individuals living with an ileostomy.</p></p>
