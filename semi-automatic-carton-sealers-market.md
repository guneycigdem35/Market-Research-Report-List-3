<p><p>Semi-automatic carton sealers come in various types such as side belt driven, top and bottom belt driven, and others. Side belt driven carton sealers use side belts to move the carton through the sealing process, ensuring a secure seal on the sides. Top and bottom belt driven carton sealers use belts on both the top and bottom of the carton to securely seal it. Other types of semi-automatic carton sealers may use different mechanisms or technologies for sealing cartons.</p></p>
