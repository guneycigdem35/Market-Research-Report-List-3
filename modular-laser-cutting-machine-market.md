<p><p>Modular laser cutting machines come in three main types: CNC (Computer Numerical Control), Manual Control, and PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) Control. CNC machines are automated and controlled by a computer, offering precise and efficient cutting capabilities. Manual control machines require the operator to manually adjust settings and guide the cutting process. PLC control machines use programmable logic controllers to automate and optimize cutting processes, offering a balance between precision and operator control. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
