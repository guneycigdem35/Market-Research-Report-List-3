<p><p>Nano paints are classified into four main types based on the type of nanoparticles used in the formulation. Nano-SiO2 paints contain silicon dioxide nanoparticles which provide enhanced durability and water resistance. Nano Silver paints utilize silver nanoparticles for their antimicrobial properties. Nano-TiO2 paints incorporate titanium dioxide nanoparticles known for their UV blocking and self-cleaning abilities. Nano-ZNO paints contain zinc oxide nanoparticles that offer enhanced UV protection and anti-corrosive properties. Each type of nano paint provides unique benefits and features for various applications.</p></p>
