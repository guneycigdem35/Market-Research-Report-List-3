<p><p>Standard stainless steel mixer taps are the most common type found in households, offering a basic design with separate hot and cold water controls. Thermostatic mixer taps have built-in temperature control to prevent scalding, making them popular in homes with young children or elderly individuals. Electronic mixer taps use sensors to control water flow and temperature, providing a touchless and hygienic option often seen in commercial settings. Self-closing mixer taps shut off automatically after a set amount of time, promoting water conservation and reducing the risk of overflow.</p></p>
