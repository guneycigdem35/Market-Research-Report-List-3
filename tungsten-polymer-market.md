Polypropylene blend is a mixture of tungsten and polypropylene, offering high tensile strength and impact resistance. Polybutene blend combines tungsten with polybutene to improve flexibility and durability. Polyurethane hybrid blends tungsten with polyurethane for enhanced chemical resistance and elasticity. Thermoplastic material mixing involves combining tungsten with various thermoplastic materials to create custom blends with specific properties for different applications.</p></p>
