There are two main types of laser resurfacing machines: ablative lasers and nonablative lasers. </p><p>Ablative lasers work by removing thin layers of skin to improve skin texture and appearance. They are more aggressive and require longer recovery time. Nonablative lasers, on the other hand, stimulate collagen production without damaging the outer skin layer. They are less invasive and have shorter downtime. Both types of lasers have their own advantages and applications in skin care treatments.</p></p>
