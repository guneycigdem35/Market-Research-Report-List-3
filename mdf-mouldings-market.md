Fire-retardant MDF is commonly used in construction projects where fire safety is a concern, while Moisture Resistant MDF is ideal for areas with high moisture levels such as bathrooms and kitchens. General MDF is widely used in furniture making, cabinetry, and interior design.</p></p>
