<p><p>Wave energy converters market consists of various types of devices such as Point Absorbers, Terminators, Attenuators, Oscillating Wave Surge, Submerged Pressure Differential, and Rotating Mass Devices. Point Absorbers harness energy through floating buoys, Terminators capture wave energy at shorelines, Attenuators utilize multiple floating segments, Oscillating Wave Surge devices extract energy through horizontal motion, Submerged Pressure Differential devices utilize pressure differences, and Rotating Mass Devices generate energy through rotating components. Each of these devices plays a unique role in harnessing wave energy for electricity generation.</p></p>
