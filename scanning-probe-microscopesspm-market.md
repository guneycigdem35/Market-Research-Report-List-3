<p><p>Scanning Probe Microscopes (SPM) are used for nanoscale imaging and manipulation. The three main types are Atomic Force Microscope (AFM), which measures forces between a probe and a sample; Laser Force Microscope (LFM), which uses laser beams to detect forces between tip and sample; and Magnetic Force Microscope (MFM), which measures magnetic forces between the probe and the sample. These SPM technologies offer high resolution imaging and precise manipulation capabilities, making them essential tools for research in fields such as materials science, biology, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
