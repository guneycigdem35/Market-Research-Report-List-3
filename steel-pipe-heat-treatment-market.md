<p><p>Steel pipe heat treatment market includes several types such as hardening, tempered, quenching, and others. Hardening involves heating the steel pipes to a high temperature and then rapidly cooling to increase hardness. Tempered steel pipes are heated to a lower temperature to reduce brittleness and improve toughness. Quenching involves cooling the steel pipes in a liquid to achieve desired properties. Other heat treatments may include annealing, normalizing, or stress relieving to alter the microstructure and properties of the steel pipes.</p></p>
