<p><p>Manual machine tool chucks are available in various types, including 2-jaw, 3-jaw, and 4-jaw chucks. </p><p>- 2-jaw chucks are designed for holding square or rectangular workpieces and offer greater stability and accuracy compared to other types. </p><p>- 3-jaw chucks are versatile and commonly used for holding round or hexagonal workpieces. They provide quick and easy centering of workpieces. </p><p>- 4-jaw chucks are ideal for holding irregularly shaped or asymmetrical workpieces and allow for more precise centering and balancing. Each type caters to specific machining needs and workpiece requirements.</p></p>
