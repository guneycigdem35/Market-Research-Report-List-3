<p><p>Standoff radiation detectors are categorized into three main market types: Gamma detection, neutron detection, and source localization. </p><p>Gamma detection systems are designed to identify and measure gamma radiation emitted from radioactive sources. Neutron detection systems are used to detect and quantify neutrons, which are commonly associated with nuclear materials. Source localization systems are used to accurately pinpoint the location of radioactive sources. Each type of detector serves a specific purpose in ensuring the safety and security of various environments from potential radiation threats.</p></p>
