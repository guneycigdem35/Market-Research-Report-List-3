<p><p>Automated dental laboratory ovens come in different types such as muffle, vacuum, infrared, microwave, and other specialized ovens. Muffle ovens are used for general dental laboratory applications, while vacuum ovens are suitable for drying and heating materials under vacuum conditions. Infrared ovens use radiation to heat materials quickly and efficiently. Microwave ovens utilize electromagnetic waves to provide rapid and even heating. Other specialized ovens cater to specific needs within the dental laboratory industry. Each type of oven offers unique benefits and advantages for different dental laboratory processes.</p></p>
