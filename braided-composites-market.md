<p><p>Braided composites market includes various types such as biaxial, triaxial, and others. Biaxial composites are woven with fibers in two directions, providing strength and stiffness in those directions. Triaxial composites are woven with fibers in three directions, offering improved structural integrity and load-bearing capabilities. Other types of braided composites may include multidirectional braids or custom braids tailored to specific applications. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of performance and versatility, catering to a wide range of industries and applications.</p></p>
