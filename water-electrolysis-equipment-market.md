<p><p>Water Electrolysis Equipment Market is segmented into two types - Traditional Alkaline Electroliser and PEM Electroliser. Traditional Alkaline Electroliser uses potassium hydroxide solution as an electrolyte, making it suitable for large-scale industrial applications. On the other hand, PEM Electroliser utilizes a proton exchange membrane as an electrolyte, making it compact, efficient, and suitable for smaller applications such as residential or portable fuel cells. Both types play a crucial role in producing hydrogen gas through electrolysis of water.</p></p>
