<p><p>Hospital ERP Software market includes two main types of systems - On Premise ERP and Cloud-based ERP. On Premise ERP systems require physical servers and infrastructure within the hospital premises, offering more control and customization but requiring higher upfront costs. Both types have their advantages and drawbacks, and hospitals choose the system that best suits their needs and preferences.</p></p>
