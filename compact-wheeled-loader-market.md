<p><p>Compact wheeled loaders are available in different market types based on their drive systems. Hydrodynamic drive loaders use a hydraulic system to control speed and power transmission. Mechanical drive loaders rely on mechanical components like gears and axles for propulsion. Electrical drive loaders use electric motors for power and torque. Each drive system has its own advantages and applications, with hydrodynamic drives offering better efficiency, mechanical drives being simpler and more cost-effective, and electrical drives providing clean and quiet operation.</p></p>
