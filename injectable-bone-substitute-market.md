<p><p>Injectable bone substitute market includes natural and synthetic options. Natural bone substitutes are made from materials such as calcium phosphate and calcium sulfate, which closely mimic the composition of human bone. Synthetic bone substitutes, on the other hand, are made from materials like hydroxyapatite and tricalcium phosphate, and are designed to provide structural support and promote bone growth. Both types of injectable bone substitutes are used in orthopedic and dental procedures to fill bone voids or defects and promote bone regeneration.</p></p>
