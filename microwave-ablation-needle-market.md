<p><p>Microwave ablation needles come in various types including conical, triangular, and oblique corrugated. Conical needles have a tapered shape, allowing for precise targeting of tissues. Triangular needles have a three-sided design which may enhance tissue penetration. Oblique corrugated needles feature a ribbed surface, potentially improving heat distribution during the ablation process. Each type offers unique benefits and may be chosen based on the specific needs of the procedure and the desired outcome.</p></p>
