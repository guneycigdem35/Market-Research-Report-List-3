<p><p>Wide-Range Vacuum Gauges can be classified into two types based on the method of measurement - Indirectly Measurement and Direct Measurement. Indirectly Measurement involves measuring the pressure inside a chamber by observing the effects of vacuum on other factors such as thermal conductivity or ionization. Direct Measurement, on the other hand, directly measures the pressure inside the chamber using physical sensors or gauges. Both types have their own advantages and applications in various industries depending on the specific requirements of the vacuum system.</p></p>
