<p><p>Dental Practice Management Software comes in two main types - On-Premise and Cloud-based. On-Premise software is installed and operated locally on the practice's servers and computers. It offers benefits like lower upfront costs, automatic updates, and the ability to access data from any device with an internet connection.</p></p>
