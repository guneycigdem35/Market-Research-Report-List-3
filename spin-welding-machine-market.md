<p><p>Spin welding machines can be classified into two main types: portable and stationary. On the other hand, stationary spin welding machines are fixed in place and typically used in manufacturing facilities for high-volume production. Both types of machines use rotational friction to join thermoplastic parts together, creating strong and durable welding bonds. Each type of machine caters to different welding needs and requirements.</p></p>
