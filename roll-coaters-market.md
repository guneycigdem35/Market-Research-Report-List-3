<p><ul><li>Hot Melt Roll Coaters</li><li>Cold Adhesive Roll Coaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roll coaters are machines used for applying coatings to various materials. Hot melt roll coaters utilize melted adhesive or coating material that is transferred onto the material's surface through a heated roller. On the other hand, cold adhesive roll coaters apply coatings using non-heated rollers and adhesive that is not melted. The market for hot melt roll coaters is growing due to their efficiency and quick drying time, while the cold adhesive roll coaters market is favored for applications where heat-sensitive materials are being coated.</p></p>
