<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Meat moisture analysis equipment market offers two main types of technology: DEXA and NIR. DEXA technology, also known as Dual-energy X-ray Absorptiometry, uses X-rays to measure the density and composition of meat products. On the other hand, NIR technology, which stands for Near Infrared Spectroscopy, analyzes the molecular structure of meat samples to determine their moisture content. Both technologies provide accurate and efficient solutions for meat moisture analysis, catering to the diverse needs of the food industry.</p></p>
