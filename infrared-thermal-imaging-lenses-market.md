<p><p>Infrared thermal imaging lenses come in three main types - Spheres, Aspheres, and Others. Spherical lenses have a uniform curvature and are commonly used in simple applications. Aspherical lenses have a more complex shape, reducing spherical aberration and improving image quality in more advanced systems. Other types of lenses may include freeform, diffractive, or cylindrical lenses, each serving different purposes based on specific imaging requirements. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
