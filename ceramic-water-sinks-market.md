<p><p>Ceramic water sinks come in various types to suit different preferences and needs. Drop-in water sinks are installed by dropping them into a pre-cut hole in the countertop. Pedestal water sinks are freestanding and have a separate pedestal for support. Top-mount water sinks are placed on top of the countertop, while under-mount water sinks are installed beneath the countertop for a seamless look. Wall-mount water sinks are mounted onto the wall, saving floor space. Other types of ceramic water sinks may include vessel sinks, console sinks, and integrated sinks.</p></p>
