<p><strong>The Automatic String Welding Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-row Welding</li><li>Double-row Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic string welding machines come in two main types: single-row welding and double-row welding. Single-row welding machines are designed to weld a single row of strings together, making them suitable for smaller scale welding projects. In contrast, double-row welding machines can weld two rows of strings simultaneously, increasing productivity and efficiency for larger welding projects. Both types of machines offer automated operation for precise and consistent welds, but double-row welding machines are preferred for higher volume production.</p></p>
