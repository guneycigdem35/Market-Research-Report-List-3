<p><strong>The Wall Mounted Ozone Disinfection Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Medium</li><li>Non-Glass Dielectric Medium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wall mounted ozone disinfection machines are available in two main market types: glass medium and non-glass dielectric medium. </p><p>Glass medium machines use glass tubes to generate ozone through corona discharge. These machines are more durable and have a longer lifespan.</p><p>Non-glass dielectric medium machines use materials like ceramic or quartz to generate ozone. They are lighter and more cost-effective, but may be less durable than glass medium machines.</p><p>Both types are effective at disinfecting air and surfaces, but have different features and benefits.</p></p>
