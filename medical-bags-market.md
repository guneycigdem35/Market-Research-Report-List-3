<p><p>Medical bags market consists of various types such as Anesthesia Breathing Bags, Bile Collection Bags, Ostomy Bags, Resuscitation Bags, Blood Bags, CAPD Bags, Enema Bags, Enteral Feeding Bags, and Urinary Collection Bags. Anesthesia Breathing Bags are used to deliver gases from anesthesia machine to the patient. Bile Collection Bags are used to collect bile from patients with a drainage catheter. Ostomy Bags are used to collect waste from a surgically created stoma. Resuscitation Bags are used for manual ventilation during emergency situations. Blood Bags are used for blood collection, storage, and administration. CAPD Bags are used for peritoneal dialysis. Enema Bags are used to administer enemas. Enteral Feeding Bags are used to deliver liquid nutrition directly into the stomach or intestines. Urinary Collection Bags are used to collect urine from patients who are unable to use the toilet.</p></p>
