<p><p>Pre-impregnated composite fibers, also known as prepreg, are advanced composite materials where a reinforcement fiber is pre-impregnated with a matrix material such as epoxy resin. This allows for easier handling and processing, as the resin is already evenly distributed on the fibers before being cured. Prepregs are commonly used in applications such as aerospace, automotive, wind energy, and sporting goods due to their high strength-to-weight ratio and excellent mechanical properties.</p><p>The Pre-impregnated Composite Fiber Market is witnessing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 13% during the forecast period.
