<p><p>Bauxite mining market types include Trihydrate or Gibbsite Bauxite, Mixed Bauxite, and Monohydrate Bauxite. Trihydrate or Gibbsite Bauxite is the most common type, containing high levels of aluminum hydroxide. Mixed Bauxite consists of a combination of different aluminum minerals, while Monohydrate Bauxite contains lower levels of aluminum hydroxide. Each type of bauxite offers varying levels of aluminum content and purity, which determines its suitability for different industrial applications such as aluminum production.</p></p>
