<p><p>Passenger lifts are broadly categorized into two types - hydraulic passenger lifts and electric passenger lifts. Hydraulic passenger lifts operate using a hydraulic system that pushes the elevator cab up and down by using fluid pressure. On the other hand, electric passenger lifts operate using an electric motor that drives a cable system to move the elevator cab. Both types of lifts have their own advantages and are used in various settings depending on factors such as building height, space availability, and budget constraints.</p></p>
