<p><ul><li>Anatomical Shoulder Prosthesis</li><li>Reverse Shoulder Prosthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anatomical Shoulder Prosthesis is a type of shoulder replacement device that mimics the natural anatomy of the shoulder joint. It is designed to replace the damaged or diseased parts of the shoulder joint with artificial components. </p><p>Reverse Shoulder Prosthesis is another type of shoulder replacement device that is specifically designed for patients with severe shoulder arthritis and rotator cuff damage. This device reverses the normal anatomy of the shoulder joint, placing a metal ball on the shoulder blade and a plastic socket on the upper arm bone.</p><p>Both types of shoulder prosthesis aim to restore function and reduce pain in patients with shoulder joint issues.</p></p>
