<p><strong>The Rigid PU Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-reactive Amine Catalysts</li><li>Reactive Amine Catalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rigid PU Catalyst Market is segmented into Non-reactive Amine Catalysts and Reactive Amine Catalysts. Non-reactive Amine Catalysts are used to accelerate the reaction rate of polyurethane production without actively participating in the chemical reaction. On the other hand, Reactive Amine Catalysts not only accelerate the reaction rate but also take part in the chemical reaction itself, leading to enhanced properties of the final polyurethane product. Both types of catalysts play a crucial role in the manufacturing of rigid polyurethane foams.</p></p>
