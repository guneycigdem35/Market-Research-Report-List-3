<p><p>Metal cans for food and beverage come in two main types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are made by welding together three separate pieces - the body, end, and lid. These cans are typically used for foods that require a high level of protection, such as canned vegetables. Two-piece cans, on the other hand, are made from just two pieces - the body and end, which are formed together in a single process. These cans are often used for beverages and canned fruits.</p></p>
