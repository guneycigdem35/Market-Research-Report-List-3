<p><ul><li>Jaffes Kinetic Test</li><li>Creatinine-PAP</li><li>ELISA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Creatinine assay kits are used for the detection and quantification of creatinine levels in biological samples. The market types include Jaffes Kinetic Test, Creatinine-PAP, and ELISA. Jaffes Kinetic Test utilizes the reaction between creatinine and picric acid to measure creatinine concentrations. Creatinine-PAP utilizes a reaction with alkaline picrate to produce a colorimetric endpoint. ELISA kits use enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays to detect and quantify creatinine levels. These different market types offer researchers various options for accurately measuring creatinine levels in biological samples.</p></p>
