The market includes absorbable bone plates, which are plates made from materials like polymers that gradually degrade and are absorbed by the body over time. Absorbable screws are used in conjunction with the plates to secure the fractured bone in place. Other resorbable fixation devices include pins, screws, and rods made from similar biocompatible materials that are gradually broken down and absorbed by the body.</p></p>
