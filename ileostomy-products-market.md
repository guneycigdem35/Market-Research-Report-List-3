<p><p>Ileostomy products market types consist of one-piece bags and two-piece bags. One-piece bags combine the skin barrier and pouch into a single unit, making them easier to use and more discreet. Two-piece bags have separate skin barriers and pouches, providing more flexibility for changing the pouch without having to remove the skin barrier. Both types offer options for individuals with an ileostomy to manage waste effectively and comfortably in their daily lives.</p></p>
