<p><p>The solvent-free packaging adhesive market can be classified into two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed and require no additional mixing before application, making them easy to use. Two-component adhesives, on the other hand, consist of two separate components that must be mixed together before application. While one-component adhesives are more convenient, two-component adhesives are often preferred for their higher strength and versatility in bonding different types of materials.</p></p>
