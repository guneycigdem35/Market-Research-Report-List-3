<p><p>A hay baler machine is a piece of agricultural equipment used to compress and bale hay into compact, easily manageable bundles for storage or transport. The machine typically picks up hay supplied by a rake, compacts it using a compressing chamber, and then ties it off with twine or wire to create a bale. Hay balers come in various types, including round balers and square balers, each offering different advantages based on the user's needs.</p><p>The global hay baler machine market is anticipated to witness significant growth during the forecast period, with a projected CAGR of 6.7%.
