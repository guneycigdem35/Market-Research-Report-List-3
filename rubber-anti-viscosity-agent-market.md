<p><p>Rubber anti viscosity agents are used to prevent the undesirable stickiness and buildup of rubber compounds during processing. The three main types of anti viscosity agents in the market are stearates, fatty acid esters, and fatty acid amides. Stearates are metal salts of fatty acids, fatty acid esters are formed by reacting fatty acids with alcohols, and fatty acid amides are derived from mixing fatty acids with amines. These agents help improve processing efficiency and product quality in the rubber industry.</p></p>
